We are a collective of travellers from around the world who aim to unite and empower the youth through music, art, play and performance.

2019

We came together to run our numerous workshops during the Guinean Carnival of Cultures. While supporting the official children's carnival stage, we also ran numerous activities and workshops for kids. At night our stage was host to a huge mix of African and European sounds and artists.

2025

We got back on the road after a pandemic induced hiatus, mobilizing materials and activities once again. We sent a container full of donations from Canada aimed at supporting families in the capital Bissau, and the people of Gantumane, a remote fishing village in the south of Guinea Bissau. Our primary goal this year was to build and install a solar powered water tower in the women's garden in Gantumane. We also ran activities and events, including kids singing and rapping competitions, arts and crafts, face painting, welding workshops, and environmental workshops.

2026

We will be hosting the Tropical Wave festival in the communities in the south of Guinea Bissau, using the event as a fundraiser and also a catalyst to deliver materials and expertise from a myriad of peoples. We hope to be able to finance the regular visiting of doctors and dentists to come visit these remote communities in the south for clinics. We are also hoping to buy a borehole drill to be able to continue our work installing water towers across the region.
